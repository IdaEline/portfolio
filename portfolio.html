FRILE

For this assignment we were asked to create a magazine with a target audience in USA and Canada. We had to produce a name, logo, front/back page, images, illustrations and a complete layout. I created a magazine that is a simplistic with some colour, focuses on the use of typography, gives the impression of exclusiveness, but are at the same time personal. Overall, I also wanted to give a positive, light and free feel to the magazine. The name “frile” comes from this idea of light and free. Colour is added to give a positive feel, handwritten fonts to give it a personal touch and large images, headlines, and generous spacing is used to show the exclusiveness of the magazine.

FARM FRESH TO YOU

For this assignment we were asked to design a new logo and a visual identity for the company “Farm fresh to you”, which planned to expand to Grimstad. It’s originally a US based company which partners with local farmers and do home deliveries of fresh vegetables. The target group for this project was the population of Grimstad, further limited to couples and couples with children as a main focus. I created a logo with a friendly and playful look to target families. The icon of the logo is a wheat field, as there are many of these in Grimstad, in-closing it in a round shape as this is a playful and friendly shape. I used warm and natural colours, colours of the field and fresh vegetables. And I choose fonts similar to does used for previous design work done for Grimstad, together with a handwritten font which added some personal touch to the design. The round shape was re-used in colours from the logo in a pattern together with graphics of fresh vegetables as a design element both on the delivery van and packaging.

THE GRIMSTAD URBAN FLOWER FESTIVAL

For this assignment we were asked to promote an annual “Urban Garden and Flower Festival” in the nearest town, Grimstad. We where asked to produce a logo, a brand manual and four touch points: a poster, a program, a t-shirt and a ticket bracelet.

To create a “Grimstad feel” over the festival I a choose a farm-like style with soft pastel colours and flowers, combined with a modern font and playful layout. The modern tone was to meet also the large population of young adults living in Grimstad, and the playfulness for families with children. I use several images of colourful spring flowers for inspiration to gardening. The logo of the festival is used in the same way on each touch-point, and several of the typography elements are also re-used. The colours from the logo is used as brand colours, and the leafs from the icon of the logo as a graphic element on the ticket bracelet. I also added a yellow line as a graphic element to the brand, in the yellow logo colour, which I use on several of the touch points. Overall this resulted in a consistent festival brand with a look and feel I believe will appeal to the intended target group, population of Grimstad.

UNGDOM I OPPDRAG

For the organization "Ungdom i Oppdrag-Oslo" I was asked to create a folder and a rollup. It was to promote the organization amongst young adults and students in Oslo. Dark blue was a colour already used a lot by the organization so I used this in combination with a light blue and yellow. This gave them a stronger brand colour scheme. I used lines and the same typeface on both designes to create conistency. I also used the same images, which show students working in the organizationand and a street in Oslo. The logo is designed by the organization.
